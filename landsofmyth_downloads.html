Lands of Myth: Downloads

Below you will find all downloads for Lands of Myth.

Rulebook and cards

The Lands of Myth rulebook and all necessary cards (provided for self printing & in www.printerstudio.com ready format). This also includes the starting region of Highgate.

Wheathall region and cards

Wheathall is one of the regions that the heroes can explore in Lands of Myth. It features new enemies to fight, new merchants, new quests and integrates with the official Stone of Life module.

Download

Ur region and cards

Ur is a two skull region and can be explored after the heroes finished a one skull region e.g. Wheathall. It features new enemies to fight, new merchants, new quests and integrates with the first part of the official Blackwall Warrens or Shores of Kanis modules.

Download

Zehir region and cards

Zehir is a two skull region and can be explored after the heroes finished the Ur region. It features new enemies to fight, new merchants, new quests and integrates with the second part of the official Shores of Kanis module.
