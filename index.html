Lands of Myth

Lands of Myth is a continuous campaign mod for Myth. Your group of heroes will venture through the world, encounter various quests, dangerous monsters and try to push back the Darkness. More...

The Deep Dark

The Deep Dark brings dungeon crawling to Myth. Heroes will explore vast ruins, overcome perilous events, face deadly monsters and hopefully make it out alive with some treasure. More...

Hero Variants

These hero variants are complete overhauls of existing heroes, coming with all hero cards and a detailed hero description sheet. More...

Individual Mods

These mods may be used to enhance normal Myth games, even without all other downloads. Note that all of these mods are also included in Lands of Myth. More...
