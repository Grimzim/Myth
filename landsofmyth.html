Lands of Myth is a continuous campaign game mod for Myth. Your group of heroes will venture through the world, encounter various quests, dangerous monsters and try to push back the Darkness. Your heroes will keep all their items and other upgrades throughout their journey. Lands of Myth adds various things to Myth: new items, mutation cards, new merchants, new hero cards to name a few. You can also use these additions in your normal Myth games i.e. without playing the continuous campaign mode.

Features

Hero Attribute Cards

Inspired by Myth: Dark Frontier, each hero comes with his own unique Attribute Card. These cards show the 4 attributes Tactics, Intellect, Cunning and Spirit and are used when performing Attributre Tests (the replacement for most Non-Combat Actions). 

Special Hero Cards

These new special hero cards (with a yellow flame) allow for additional hero progression and are added to the hero deck as a special reward, usually when finishing a region.

Injury and Fatigue Hero Cards

Injury hero cards usually come into play if a hero is reduced to zero vitality. Fatigue cards usually come into play when a hero has to perform a attribute test and wants to increase his chances for success. Both card types are added temporarily to the hero deck until the hero can get rid of them. 

Lair Cards

These cards collect the lair information on a separate card instead of the minion backside. Apart from having an easier reference, they can also be used if you want to generate a random lair. 

Mutation Cards

These cards come in two variants: lair and captain mutation cards. Both card types grant enhanced attributes or new abilities for lairs and captains and thus make them more random (and harder to beat).

New Items

White item cards allow for smoother item progression for your heroes. Each region usually also brings additional items that can only be found in the quests of that region.

Regions

A region is a part of the Myth world where the heroes experience their adventures. Each region comes with a detailed setup, e.g. regarding what items can be found, what merchants can be encountered and how the influence of the Darkness can be reduced by the heroes. Regions combine modules, quests and even dungeons (if you play The Deep Dark) in a coherent way.

Darkness Influence

The Darkness Influence Tracker is used to measure how active the Darkness is in a particular region. The tracker starts at 10 and any heroic deeds the heroes accomplish (completing quests, finishing module acts …) will push the Darkness’ influence back. Heroes may gain special rewards when they push the Darkness back or unlock new quests or merchants. However, the Darkness may also fight back and try to overpower the heroes...
