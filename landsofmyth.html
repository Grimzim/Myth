Lands of Myth is a continuous campaign mod for Myth. Your group of heroes will venture through the world, encounter various quests, dangerous monsters and try to push back the Darkness. Your heroes will keep all their items and other upgrades throughout their journey. Lands of Myth adds various news things to Myth: new items, lair and captain mutation cards, new merchants, new hero cards - to name a just a few. You can also use these additions in your normal Myth games i.e. without playing a continuous campaign.

How to play

  1. SELECT YOUR HEROES - to start your journey into the Lands of Myth you pick your group of heroes as normal and start your journey by exploring your first region!
  2. EXPLORE REGIONS - a region is a part of the Myth world. Each region will come with detailed setup instruction regarding what items can be found, what merchants can be encountered and what quests are available. Quest in Lands ofMyth are not simple quest cards, but fully detailed descriptions: they come with short story texts and detailed setup and special rule instructions for each step of the quest. In addition, each region integrates with one of the official or fan-made Myth modules. Your heroes can also explore a dungeon if you wish, integrating seamlessly with The Deep Dark.  
  3. FIGHT THE DARKNESS - to travel to another region the heroes have to fight the Darkness and reduce its influence over the land. All completed quests, module acts or successful adventures in a dungeon will reduce the Darkness influence of the region. This will unlock rewards for the heroes but beware: the Darkness will fight back!

Details

Regions

A region is a part of the Myth world where the heroes experience their adventures. Each region comes with a detailed setup, e.g. regarding what items can be found, what merchants can be encountered and how the influence of the Darkness can be reduced by the heroes. Regions combine modules, quests and even dungeons (if you play The Deep Dark) in a coherent way.

Darkness Influence

The Darkness Influence tracker is used to measure how active the Darkness is in a particular region. The tracker starts at 10 and any heroic deeds the heroes accomplish (completing quests, finishing module acts …) will push the Darkness’ influence back. Heroes may gain special rewards when they push the Darkness back or unlock new quests or merchants. However, the Darkness may also fight back and try to overpower the heroes...

Village Actions

Between quests heroes will often find themselves in a village or town that is central to the region. This is their starting point to rest between adventures, buy and sell items and look out for new quests. Depending on the Darkness Influence new quest or travel options may become available, allowing the heroes to further venture into the Lands of Myth...

Quests

Each region comes with a set of fully detailed quests. These are described on multiple pages and provide not only detailed setup instructions and special rules but also come with short story texts. All quests are thematically integrated into the region. Fulfilling a quest will not only grant the heroes Serendipity and Gold but will also reduce the Darkness Influence in that region (unlokcing special rewards or opening up new quests). 

Features

Hero Attribute Cards

Inspired by Myth: Dark Frontier, each hero comes with his own unique Attribute Card. These cards show 4 attributes: Tactics, Intellect, Cunning and Spirit and are used when performing Attribute Tests (a replacement for most Non-Combat Actions). 

Special Hero Cards

These new special hero cards (with a yellow flame) allow for additional hero progression and are added to the hero deck as a special reward, usually when finishing a region.

Injury and Fatigue Hero Cards

Injury hero cards usually come into play if a hero is reduced to zero vitality. Fatigue cards usually come into play when a hero has to perform a attribute test and wants to increase his chances for success. Both card types are added temporarily to the hero deck until the hero can get rid of them. 

Lair Cards

These cards collect the lair information on a separate card instead of the minion backside. Apart from having an easier reference, they can also be used if you want to generate a random lair. 

Lair Mutation Cards

Lair Mutation Cards grant enhanced attributes or new abilities to Lairs and thus make them more random (and harder to beat).

Captain Mutation Cards

Captain Mutation Cards grant enhanced attributes or new abilities to Captains and thus make them more random (and harder to beat).

New Monsters

New and dangerous monsters will make short work of any unprepared group of heroes.

New Items

White item cards allow for smoother item progression. In addition, regions often come with additional items that can only be found in the quests of that region.
