Lands of Myth is a continuous campaign game mode for Myth. Your group of heroes will venture through the world, encounter various quests, dangerous monsters and try to push back the Darkness. Your heroes will keep all their items and other upgrades throughout their journey. Lands of Myth adds various things to Myth: new items, mutation cards, new merchants, new hero cards to name a few. You can also use these additions in your normal Myth games i.e. without playing the continuous campaign mode.

Hero Attribute Cards

Inspired by Myth: Dark Frontier, each hero comes with his own unique Attribute Card. These cards show the 4 attributes Tactics, Intellect, Cunning and Spirit and are used when performing Attributre Tests (the replacement for most Non-Combat Actions). 

Special Hero Cards

These new special hero cards (with a yellow flame) allow for additional hero progression.
