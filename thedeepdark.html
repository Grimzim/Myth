The Deep Dark brings dungeon crawling to Myth. It is an exiting new way to play and combines exploration with the typical Myth combat. Heroes will explore vast ruins, overcome perilous events, face deadly monsters and hopefully make it out alive with some treasure. You can play The Deep Dark standalone or seamlessly integrate it with the continuous campaign mod Lands of Myth.

How to play

  1. SELECT YOUR HEROES - to start your journey into The Deep Dark just pick your group of heroes and choose a dungeon to explore!
  2. SELECT A QUEST - each dungeon comes with a set of special dungeon quests you can try to accomplish. Before venturing into a dungeon you have to select a quest and follow the setup instructions.
  3. EXPLORE THE DUNGEON - The dungeon itself is represented by a dedicated deck of cards, giving a birds-eye view of your surroundings. Step by step your group of heroes will move through monster infested tunnels, constantly trying to overcome perilious events and desperately trying to finish your chosen quest. If you play Lands of Myth dungeons seamlessly integrate into the regions your are playing.  

Features

Hero Attribute Cards

Inspired by Myth: Dark Frontier, each hero comes with his own unique Attribute Card. These cards show 4 attributes: Tactics, Intellect, Cunning and Spirit and are used to overcome the various Dungeon Events your group of heroes will encounter in The Deep Dark.

Injury and Fatige Hero Cards

Injury hero cards usually come into play if a hero is reduced to zero vitality. Fatigue cards usually come into play when a hero has to perform a attribute test and wants to increase his chances for success. Both card types are added temporarily to the hero deck until the hero can get rid of them.
